## Overview
This Tableau project monitors core financial KPIs (Revenue, Profit Margin, ROI, Cash Flow) and investment performance across regions, asset types, and categories to support strategic decision-making for finance and leadership teams.

## Sample Dataset (key fields)
- Date, Month, Quarter, Year
- Investment Category, Investment Type, Asset Type, Risk Level
- Region
- Revenue, Expenses, Profit, Profit Margin %
- Investment Amount, ROI %, Portfolio Value
- Budgeted Revenue, Actual Revenue, Variance %
- Market Trend Indicator

## KPIs & Calculations
- Total Revenue, Total Expenses, Total Profit
- Profit Margin %, ROI %, Portfolio Growth %
- Budget Variance %
- Investment Performance Score
- Category-wise Profitability, Highest ROI Asset

## Dashboard Views
- KPI cards for Revenue, Profit, ROI, Cash Flow
- Profit by Investment Category (bar)
- Revenue by Region (column)
- Revenue & Profit Trend over time (line)
- Asset Type Distribution (donut)
- Region vs ROI (heatmap)
- Investment Amount vs ROI (scatter)
- Financial summary table with filters (Region, Category, Asset Type, Risk Level)

## Insights Enabled
Identify top-performing investments, regional profitability patterns, budget vs actual gaps, and risk-adjusted ROI trends for portfolio optimization.
